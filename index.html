PV simulation on hybrid meshes

I built complex and elegant hybrid meshes of PV device, and showed the efficiency and benefits of using such configuration.

Multiscale features

Realistic ultra thin film solar cells simulation that involve multiscale features (nanometers layers surrounded by tens of microns layers).

Light-trapping simulation in realistic solar cells

Perfect topography construction from in-situ AFM data, computation of light trapping in such device, validation against laboratory measures.

Design optimisation

Finding optimal texturation in order to maximize the absorption capability of the device. Statistical learning methods mainly used (see EGO / Bayesian optimization).

MHM-DGTD for Maxwell's Equations

Developpement of a MHM-DGTD code for 2D TD Maxwell's equations. Developpement of the coarse/fine two level meshes algorithm.

Advanced mesh partitionning

Advanced strategies to reduce load balancing for complex simulations (local refinement, hybrid meshes, local observables).

Hybrid mesh generator

Toolbox implementation for hybrid mesh generation of complex configurations. Based on gmsh mesh generator.

DIOGENeS software

Developpement / Maintenement of the nanophotonic solver DIOGENeS.
